Portable Power, Anywhere

Carry-on kit with 40W foldable panel and ~100Wh battery. Power lights, radios, GPS, and phones. Deploy in minutes for mining, security, and field ops.

Technical Specifications

  • Case: Waterproof, crush-resistant
  • Solar Panel: 40W foldable; ~4–5 h full charge in good sun
  • Battery: ~100Wh (≈8,300mAh @12V class), USB & 12V I/O
  • Primary Output: 12V DC port (up to 6A)
  • Panel Outputs: USB-A + USB-C for direct device charging while charging the battery
  • Floodlight: ~900 lm, 120° beam, ~9–9.5 h runtime per full charge
  • Deployment Time: Under 2 minutes
  • Use Cases: Remote lighting, device charging, field comms, recon gear, clinics

Prototype measurements; minor changes may occur in production.
